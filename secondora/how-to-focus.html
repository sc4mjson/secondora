How to Stay Focused

Maintaining focus can be challenging, but using structured techniques helps you work efficiently and reduce distractions.

Tips to Improve Focus

  • Create a minimal distraction workspace.
  • Turn off notifications on your phone or computer.
  • Set clear goals for each session.
  • Use time blocks and short breaks.
  • Practice mindfulness or breathing exercises to reset focus.
