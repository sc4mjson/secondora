Pomodoro Technique Guide

The Pomodoro Technique is a time management method using 25-minute focus sessions followed by short breaks. It helps improve productivity, focus, and reduces mental fatigue.

How to Use Pomodoro

  1. Choose a task to focus on.
  2. Set your timer for 25 minutes.
  3. Work until the timer rings.
  4. Take a 5-minute break.
  5. Repeat 4 Pomodoros, then take a longer 15-30 minute break.

Benefits

  • Boosts concentration
  • Reduces procrastination
  • Helps track progress
